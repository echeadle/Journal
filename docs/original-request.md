# Keep a 5 day food, energy and mood journal

 I am looking for trends throughout your day.  Patients typically will describe their mood (depressed, anxious, flat, happy, etc) and then I ask you to give it a score of 1-5 (1 being low and 5 being extreme).  I recommend making a note during the following times:

## ** Upon waking (energy and mood).**

## ** After you break your fast.**

## ** Around noon or after lunch.##

## ** After dinner**

## ** Before you go to bed.**

For food what I really would like to see is when you first eat (types of food, calories are not as important to me) and then when you stop eating for the day/evening.
